Taylor Will

My journey into data began about two years ago when I was searching for a new career in tech, looking for a way to contribute meaningfully. I came across a quote that resonated with me: “If you love coding and building things, become a software engineer. If you want to drive change and make an impact, get into data.” That advice stuck with me, and I realized that my true interest lay in data analysis and data science.

Since then, I've immersed myself in learning the essential skills needed to excel in this field. I've completed numerous online courses to build a solid foundation in mathematics, coding, and data visualization. However, I believe that the best learning happens through hands-on experience. Working on projects has allowed me to apply these techniques in real-world scenarios, and I’ve found self-directed learning to be an essential and ongoing part of my growth.

Outside of work, I love exploring the natural beauty of Vancouver Island with my beautiful wife. Whether it's backcountry snowboarding in the winter, sea kayaking in the warmer months, or free diving year-round, I’m always excited for the next adventure.
