Description

This dashboard can be used by someone looking to rent an airbnb as well as someone looking at the feasibility of starting their own airbnb. We can look at how the average prices change by neighbourhood as well as by time of year and number of bedrooms in the rental.

Findings

Summer rental prices average about 1.5 times the winter prices, with the exception of Christmas time which brings a modest increase to average rental prices. In most locations the price of the airbnb scales fairly linearly with the number of rooms, however the average price by neighbourhood actually varies quite a bit. The most expensive neighbourhoods are well over twice as expensive as the less expensive neighbourhoods.
